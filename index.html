Community School-An idea whose time has come


A community school is an innovative platform which uses "community involvement" as a tool, not only to provide quality and holistic education to every child of the community but also focuses on the immense potential that the community have and transform that potential into real practise through the process of "community involvement" which will results into a much better and healthier environment in the community.

Community school is not owned/run by a particular person but for the community, of the community & by the community. After it's successful establishment, achieving it's primary goal of providing quality & holistic education to the children, it will act as a pillar for the government and other schools and educators in the community by developing it as a "Resource center for quality & holisitc education" and later it will also be developed as community center for public learning.

Why "community" school?

To provide the quality & holistic education to every child of the community for all class even in small town and villages

To make school an integral part of the community, not an isolated system, within the wall

To transform the immense potential of the community into real practice

Provides a platform in the community to directly participate in the school activities by sharing their experiences, ideas, skills or learning various skills so that people get exposure & satisfaction

Organize distinct programs & fellowships to engage all the age-groups in the community

Set a new standard of school education, partly by showing what can be done quickly and effectively in a short span of time and partly by changing the notion about nature and management of schools.
