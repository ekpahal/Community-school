Community School-An idea whose time has come


A community school is an innovative platform which uses "community involvement" as a tool, not only to provide quality and holistic education to every child of the community but also focuses on the immense potential that the community have and transform that potential into real practise through the process of "community involvement" which will results into a much better and healthier environment in the community. Community school is not owned/run by a particular person but for the community, of the community & by the community. After it's successful establishment, achieving it's primary goal of providing quality & holistic education to the children, it will act as a pillar for the government and other schools and educators in the community by developing it as a "Resource center for qulaity & holisitc education" and later it will also be developed as community center for public learning.
